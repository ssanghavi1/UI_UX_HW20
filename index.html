Problem Statement

Endangered Species International was designed to raise awareness and encourage donations for endangered species. We have observed that ESI isn’t providing traceability of donor funds, which is causing potential donors to distrust the organization and its mission.

Paper Prototype

Paper prototypes were helpful in helping us understand the basic navigation and task flow of our user base.

Digital Wireframes

Low fidelity digital wireframes allowed us to further our iterative design process by adding interactions and complex functionalities to test.

High Fidelity Prototype

Our final prototype reflected our custom style guide, included fully functional website content and pages, and underwent A/B testing.

Final Prototype

Our final design for ESI reflected an organized, functional, and updated website that will enable them to increase donations, site traffic, and their donor base.
